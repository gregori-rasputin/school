Urban Growth and Water Scarcity in Delhi

Introduction

Delhi, the capital city of India, faces severe water scarcity issues exacerbated by rapid urban growth. Factors such as increased demand, pollution, and infrastructure challenges have significantly impacted water sources and quality.

Visualizations

Proposed Solutions

  • Rainwater Harvesting: Collect and store rainwater for future use.
  • Water Recycling: Treat and reuse wastewater.
  • Efficient Water Management: Implement modern techniques to reduce water waste.

Water Purification Methods

  • Boiling: Heat water to kill pathogens.
  • Filtration: Use filters to remove impurities.
  • Chlorination: Add chlorine to disinfect water.
  • UV Treatment: Use ultraviolet light to eliminate bacteria and viruses.
